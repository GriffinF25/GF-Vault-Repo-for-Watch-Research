# GF Vault Domain Overview

**Domain:** GF Vault — Premium Watch Secondary Market Trading  
**Status:** Active  
**Owner:** Griffin Fletcher  
**Created:** 2026-07-12

## Strategic Focus

GF Vault acquires premium watches at wholesale/below-market prices and resells at profit. The business is disciplined, data-driven, and capital-protective.

**Why:** Build wealth through intelligent sourcing and systematic trading rather than collecting for consumption.

## Business Model

- **Sourcing:** Weekly market research identifies opportunities below baseline
- **Acquisition:** Negotiate 10-15% below asking price; disciplined pass on marginal deals
- **Holding:** 4-6 weeks typical; rapid turnover to compound capital
- **Resale:** Primary venues are eBay and Chrono24; venue chosen per reference liquidity
- **Learning:** Every transaction feeds back into pricing knowledge for better future decisions

## Key Decisions

1. **Capital protection first principle** — Only pursue deals with clear 15%+ margin or $500+ absolute profit
2. **Data-driven pricing** — Use Watch Pricing Genius; don't rely on intuition or asking prices
3. **Systematic sourcing** — Weekly market research agent identifies deals; not opportunistic hunting
4. **Record everything** — Transaction history becomes the highest-confidence evidence for future analyses
