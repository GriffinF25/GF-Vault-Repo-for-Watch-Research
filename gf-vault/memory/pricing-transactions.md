**Watch:** Rolex Submariner 116610 | 40mm | Steel | Glidelock bracelet

- Seller: Private collector
- Asking price: $9,200
- Negotiated price: $8,800
- Inbound shipping: $65
- Taxes/fees: $120
- Total invested: $8,985

**CONDITION & SET**
- Condition: Very Good (light polishing, one small scratch on crystal)
- Box: Yes
- Papers: Yes (2018 warranty)
- Links: Complete, 7.5" sized
- Warranty card: Yes
- Service history: 2023 by authorized dealer
- Noted issues: Bezel insert slightly worn, needs eventual replacement

**SALE**
- Sale date: 2026-06-10
- Marketplace: Chrono24
- Gross sale price: $9,600
- Selling platform fees (8%): $768
- Payment processing (3%): $288
- Shipping/insurance: $45
- Authentication: $0 (not required via Chrono24)
- Total selling costs: $1,101
- Net proceeds: $8,499

**PROFITABILITY**
- Days held: 26
- Total profit: -$486 ❌ (Loss)
- ROI: -5.4%
- $/day profit: -$18.70

**KEY OBSERVATIONS**
- Market was softer than expected on this reference in June
- VG condition reflected in lower sale price
- Should have negotiated harder—$800 asking was already favorable
- Lesson: Submariner 116610 market sensitive to service history; recently serviced watches worth extra negotiation

## How to Use This Data

1. **When pricing a similar watch:** Check actual transaction history first—this is highest-weight evidence
2. **Refine negotiation ranges:** Compare asking prices to actual negotiated prices
3. **Identify market shifts:** Look for trends in sale prices over time
4. **Estimate holding costs:** Use actual days held and carrying costs
5. **Evaluate marketplace choice:** Compare net proceeds by platform
6. **Learn from results:** Review "KEY OBSERVATIONS" section for lessons
