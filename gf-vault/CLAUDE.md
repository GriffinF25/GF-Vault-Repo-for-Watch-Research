## Domain Overview

GF Vault acquires premium watches at wholesale/below-market prices and resells them for profit. Focus: capital protection first, disciplined sourcing, systematic pricing intelligence.

**Manages:** Market research & deal sourcing | Pricing analysis & valuation | Acquisition & negotiation | Inventory & resale operations | Performance tracking

## Core Strategy

1. **Weekly market research:** Identify wholesale opportunities (>5-10% below baseline or 15%+ margin potential)
2. **Smart acquisition:** Use Watch Pricing Genius for disciplined analysis; negotiate 10-15% below asking
3. **Rapid resale:** Target 4–6 week holding periods; focus on liquid references
4. **Continuous learning:** Record all transactions; improve analysis over time

**Success Metrics:** Consistent profit margins, negotiation conversion rates, minimal holding period extension

## Decision-Making

1. Check current market baseline (from [[watch-pricing-knowledge]] or latest research report)
2. Run Watch Pricing Genius analysis for the specific reference
3. Assess profit potential: Must be >$500 and ≥15% margin OR obvious below-market opportunity
4. Document decision in [[pricing-transactions]] with actual outcome
5. Update market knowledge base if new insights emerge
