### Rolex Submariner 116610 (Ceramic Bezel, Glidelock)

**Reference:** 116610  
**Status:** In production | **Common Variation:** With Glidelock bracelet (post-2010)

### Market Behavior
- Typical asking price: $8,500–$12,000
- Realistic sold price: $7,800–$9,500 (recent sales, steel on bracelet)
- Most common sale condition: Very Good to Excellent (modern production, well-maintained)
- Bracelet vs. strap: Steel bracelet commands premium, typically +$800–$1,200

### Liquidity
- **Rating:** Very liquid
- **Evidence:** Multiple sales weekly across major platforms
- **Time on market:** 1–3 weeks typical
- **Seasonal patterns:** Slightly higher demand Q4, lower in summer

### Common Negotiation Range
- Asking ($9,500) to opening offer: typically -10% to -15% ($8,000–$8,600)
- Opening to strong offer: typically +3% to +7% ($8,300–$9,000)
- Glidelock bracelet loss: -$800–$1,200 vs. Submariner link bracelet version

### Notes
- Service history significant; recently serviced watches sell faster
- Dial condition highly visible in photos; slight damage affects value disproportionately
- Bezel insert condition matters for asking price; replacement costs $400–$600
